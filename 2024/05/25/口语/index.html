点餐类口语

1. Hello, can we have a table for two, please? 你好，请问我们可以订一个两人桌吗？
2. Do you have a reservation? 您预订了吗？
3. Would you like to see a menu? 您想看看菜单吗？
4. Can I get you something to drink? 您想喝点什么？
5. What are today’s specials? 今天有什么特别推荐菜吗？
6. Could you tell me more about this dish? 能告诉我更多关于这道菜吗？
7. I’ll have the fish and chips, please. 我要一份炸鱼薯条。
8. Can I have mine without onions? 可以不加洋葱吗？
9. Is this dish suitable for vegetarians? 这道菜适合素食者吗？
10. Does this contain any nuts? 这个里面有坚果吗？
11. Can I get that to go, please? 请问我可以外带吗？
12. I’d like to order a dessert. 我想点一份甜点。
13. What desserts do you recommend? 你推荐哪些甜点？
14. We’ll have one of each, please. 我们每种都要一份，请。
15. Can you split the bill for us? 能为我们分开账单吗？
16. Do you accept credit cards? 你们接受信用卡吗？
17. Is service included in the bill? 账单里包含服务费吗？
18. Could we have some extra napkins, please? 能给我们多些餐巾纸吗？
19. Can I have some more water, please? 能再来点水吗？
20. I’d like a glass of house red, please. 我想要一杯红葡萄酒。
21. Could we get some bread for the table? 能给我们送点面包吗？
22. How spicy is this dish? 这道菜有多辣？
23. I’m allergic to shellfish, can you accommodate that? 我对贝类过敏，你们能注意一下吗？
24. Can we get the check, please? 能给我们结账吗？
25. Can we move to that table? 我们能移到那张桌子吗？
26. What do you recommend for first-time visitors? 你推荐给第一次来的游客什么？
27. I’d like to start with the soup of the day. 我想先来一份今日特制的汤。
28. Can I make a reservation for tomorrow evening? 我能预订明晚的晚餐吗？
